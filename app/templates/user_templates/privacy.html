Privacy & Cookies Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

This Privacy & Cookies Policy outlines how QuickRides collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email, phone number, CNIC, and payment details when you register or book a ride. We also collect non-personal browser data through cookies.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to manage bookings, communicate with you, process payments, and enhance your experience on our website.

3. Cookies Usage

Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve website performance. You can control cookie settings in your browser at any time.

4. Data Security

All personal data is stored securely. We use SSL encryption and limit access to authorized personnel only.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared with trusted service providers only to fulfill bookings or legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Effective date: July 14, 2025
