Quick Answers for Quick Rides

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know about renting with Quick Rides.

1. What documents do I need to rent a car?

You’ll need a valid CNIC, a driving license, and a contact number. For international users, a passport and international driving permit are required.

2. Do you offer self-drive and with-driver options?

Yes! You can choose between self-drive and chauffeur-driven rides based on your convenience and preference.

3. How do I book a car?

You can browse available vehicles on our website, select your dates, and book instantly. Alternatively, contact our support team for assistance.

4. Are your services available nationwide?

Yes, Quick Rides operates in major cities across Pakistan, including Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and more.

5. What if the car breaks down during my trip?

Our 24/7 roadside assistance ensures you're never stuck. Call our support team, and we'll guide you through the process.

6. What are your payment options?

We accept payments via bank transfer, cash, Easypaisa/JazzCash, and online card payments.

7. Is there a mileage limit?

Each vehicle comes with a defined mileage limit. Extra charges may apply for exceeding the set mileage. Check details at the time of booking.

8. Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 12 hours in advance. Terms may apply based on the vehicle and location.

9. Do you provide vehicles for events and corporate bookings?

Absolutely! We cater to weddings, events, business trips, and long-term rentals. Reach out to our corporate team for tailored solutions.

10. How can I become a Quick Rides driver?

Visit our "Become A Driver" page, fill in the application form, and we’ll review and get in touch with you shortly.
